🧮 Newton's Laws & Key Equations

First Law (Inertia)

An object at rest stays at rest; an object in motion stays in motion at constant velocity, unless acted upon by a net force.

Second Law

ΣF = ma

Third Law

For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction: F₁₂ = -F₂₁

Weight

W = mg

Normal Force

N = mg cos θ (on incline)

Friction

f = μN (kinetic/static)
