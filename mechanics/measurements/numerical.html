Problem 1 Easy

A student measures the sides of a rectangular block as l = 15.12 cm, w = 3.45 cm, and h = 1.78 cm. Calculate the volume of the block, expressing the answer with the correct number of significant figures.

Solution

Step 1: Identify the operation and rule

The volume V is calculated by multiplication: V = l Г— w Г— h. The rule for multiplication is that the result must have the same number of significant figures as the measurement with the fewest significant figures.

Step 2: Count significant figures

  • l = 15.12 cm has 4 significant figures.
  • w = 3.45 cm has 3 significant figures.
  • h = 1.78 cm has 3 significant figures.

The result must be rounded to 3 significant figures.

Step 3: Calculate the volume

V = 15.12 cm Г— 3.45 cm Г— 1.78 cm = 92.78592 cmВі

Step 4: Round to the correct number of significant figures

Rounding 92.78592 to 3 significant figures gives 92.8.

Final Answer: The volume is 92.8 cmВі.

Problem 2 Easy

The speed of light is approximately 3.00 Г— 10вҒё m/s. Convert this speed to kilometers per hour (km/h).

Solution

Step 1: Set up the conversion factors

  • Meters to kilometers: 1 km = 1000 m
  • Seconds to hours: 1 hour = 3600 s

Step 2: Perform the conversion

Speed = (3.00 Г— 10вҒё m/s) Г— (1 km / 1000 m) Г— (3600 s / 1 hour)

The units of meters and seconds cancel out, leaving km/hour.

Speed = (3.00 Г— 10вҒё Г— 3600) / 1000 km/h

Speed = 10.8 Г— 10вҒё km/h

Step 3: Express in proper scientific notation

Move the decimal to get 1.08 Г— 10вҒ№ km/h.

Final Answer: The speed of light is 1.08 Г— 10вҒ№ km/h.

Problem 5 Medium

The voltage across a resistor is V = (10.0 Вұ 0.2) V and the current is I = (2.50 Вұ 0.05) A. Calculate the resistance R (R = V/I) and its percentage error.

Solution

Step 1: Calculate the mean resistance

R_mean = 10.0 V / 2.50 A = 4.00 О©

Step 2: Calculate the percentage error

For division, percentage errors add up. % Error in V = (0.2/10.0)*100% = 2%. % Error in I = (0.05/2.50)*100% = 2%.

Problem 7 Hard

The percentage error in P is the sum of the percentage errors of each variable multiplied by the magnitude of its power.

% Error in P = 3*(% Error in a) + 2*(% Error in b) + (1/2)*(% Error in c) + 1*(% Error in d)

Final Answer: The percentage error in P is 13%.
