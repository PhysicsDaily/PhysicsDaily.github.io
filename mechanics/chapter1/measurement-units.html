Units and Standards

Understanding the International System of Units, prefixes, and measurement standards

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Identify the seven SI base units and their symbols
  • Use metric prefixes correctly in calculations
  • Convert between different units using conversion factors
  • Understand the importance of measurement standards
  • Apply unit conversions to solve practical problems

📏 The International System of Units (SI)

SI Base Units

Quantity Unit Symbol
Length meter m
Mass kilogram kg
Time second s
Electric Current ampere A
Temperature kelvin K
Amount of Substance mole mol
Luminous Intensity candela cd

Common SI Prefixes

Large Numbers

  • kilo (k) = 10³
  • mega (M) = 10⁶
  • giga (G) = 10⁹
  • tera (T) = 10¹²

Small Numbers

  • milli (m) = 10⁻³
  • micro (μ) = 10⁻⁶
  • nano (n) = 10⁻⁹
  • pico (p) = 10⁻¹²

💡 Example: Unit Conversion

Convert 2.5 kilometers to meters:

2.5 km × (1000 m / 1 km) = 2500 m
