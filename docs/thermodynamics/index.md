## Key Concepts Preview

!!! info "Fundamental Laws"
    **Heat Transfer:**
    $$Q = mc\Delta T$$
    
    **First Law of Thermodynamics:**
    $$\Delta U = Q - W$$
    
    **Ideal Gas Law:**
    $$PV = nRT$$
    
    **Entropy:**
    $$\Delta S = \int \frac{dQ}{T}$$
