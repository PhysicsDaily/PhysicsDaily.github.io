# Physics Resources

Here is a curated list of resources to help you on your physics journey.

---

## :material-book-open-variant: Books

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **University Physics**

    ---

    The classic textbook by Sears and Zemansky covering all major physics topics.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/University-Physics-Modern-15th/dp/0135159555){: target="_blank" }

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **Concepts of Physics**

    ---

    H.C. Verma's excellent book for building strong fundamentals.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Concept-Physics-Part-1-Verma/dp/8177091875){: target="_blank" }

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **The Feynman Lectures**

    ---

    Richard Feynman's legendary physics lectures, available free online.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: Read Free Online](https://www.feynmanlectures.caltech.edu/){: target="_blank" }

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **Physics (Vol 1 & 2)**

    ---

    By Halliday, Resnick, & Krane. A rigorous and comprehensive treatment of physics.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Physics-Vol-1-Resnick-Halliday/dp/8126510889){: target="_blank" }

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **Fundamentals of Physics**

    ---

    By Halliday, Resnick, & Walker. The standard introductory text for many university courses.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Fundamentals-Physics-David-Halliday/dp/111823071X){: target="_blank" }

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **Problems in General Physics**

    ---

    By I.E. Irodov. Famous for its challenging problems, ideal for competitive exam prep.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Problems-General-Physics-I-Irodov/dp/8174730129){: target="_blank" }

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **Intro to Classical Mechanics**

    ---

    By David Morin. Known for its difficult problems and clear explanations.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Introduction-Classical-Mechanics-Problems-Solutions/dp/0521876222){: target="_blank" }

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **200 Puzzling Physics Problems**

    ---

    By Gn√§dig, Honyek, and Riley. A collection of intriguing and thought-provoking problems.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Puzzling-Physics-Problems-Hints-Solutions/dp/0521774802){: target="_blank" }

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **Introduction to Electrodynamics**

    ---

    By David J. Griffiths. The standard undergraduate text for electrodynamics.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Introduction-Electrodynamics-David-J-Griffiths/dp/1108420419){: target="_blank" }

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **Classical Mechanics**

    ---

    By John R. Taylor. A clear and intuitive approach to advanced mechanics.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Classical-Mechanics-John-R-Taylor/dp/189138922X){: target="_blank" }

-   :material-book:{ .lg .middle } **A Brief History of Time**

    ---

    By Stephen Hawking. A landmark volume in popular science.

    [:octicons-arrow-right-24: View on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Brief-History-Time-Stephen-Hawking/dp/0553380168){: target="_blank" }
